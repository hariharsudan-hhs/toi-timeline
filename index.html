Mr. Siva Meiyappan

Times Of India

1990 - 1993

Entered into India's media conglomerate organization - Bennett, Coleman & Company Limited (The Times of India Group) - Ahmedabad unit as Executive in Response Department.

1993

Moved to Chennai establishment, served in Operation Department till 2002.

2002

Elevated as Senior Manager - Response Development , headed Government vertical. Brought out multi booklets on variety of subjects. Notables are Connecting to the world booklets (3 consecutive years) on BSNL.

2003

Brought out unique booklet titled " Tamil Nadu Police - A Role Model ". Appreciated by then Hon'ble Chief Minister Dr J Jayalalithaa

2005

Brought out unique booklet on Rainwater Harvesting - a unique initiative by the Tamil Nadu Government - A pet project of the CM Dr J Jayalalithaa. Later, TN Rainwater initiative became in CBSE text books. Participated international media conferences in 15 countries from the year 2005 (Australia, New Zealand, Austria, Germany, Italy, Czech Republic, Morocco, Japan, Thailand, Greece, China, Philippines, Singapore, Malaysia and Turkey).

2006

Maritime Industry Connect - Mariners / A coffee table book and 4 pages special features in The Economic Times - All India editions

2007

Trailblazers of Tami Nadu - Inspirational coffee table book featuring 19 TN entrepreneurs from different fields and 4 pages post event feature brought with The Economic Times. Released by the then Hon'ble Chief Minister Dr Kalaignar.

2008

The Times of India - Chennai Edition Launch - A core team member. Launched the edition successfully, brought then Hon'ble Chief Minister Dr Kalaignar M Karunanidhi to The Times of India Chennai office to launch the edition (picture available) 13th April 2008.

2009

Brought out a special feature in Madras University Sesquiccentnial years completion.

2010

World Classical Tamil Conference: Brought out features in The Times of India. Brought out a unique books on Tamil Nadu Police Sesquiccentnial years.

2010 - 2012

Appointed as Senate Members of State Owned Manonmaniam Sundaranar University by The Chancellor and Governor of Tamil Nadu (Surjit Singh Barnala).

2013

Appointed as Planning Board Member of State Owned Bharatiar University - Coimbatore by the then Chancellor & Governor of Tamil Nadu Dr Rossiah

2014

Brought out Industry Connect - A Coffee Table Book titled " Navigators - II" launched at the SICCI shipping industry conclave.

2015

Global Investors Meet - 2015 : Brought out special feature in The Economic Times - All India editions.

2019

World Shipping Forum - in association with IMEI, brought out "Propellers" a coffee table book compilation of Indian Shipping & Maritime Industry players - unveiled by Hon'ble Union Shipping Minister Mr Nitin Kadkiri.
